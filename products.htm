Products

All product descriptions and luxury formulas on Cleopatra’s Beauty Bar are protected © Cleopatra’s Beauty Bar 2025. Our signature blends are salon‑exclusive ® formulations crafted for premium results.

  1. Hydrating Argan Shampoo — A moisture-rich formula infused with argan oil to cleanse gently while restoring softness and shine.
  2. Keratin Repair Conditioner — Strengthens damaged strands with keratin proteins, leaving hair smoother, healthier, and easier to manage.
  3. Curl Defining Leave‑In — Enhances natural curl patterns, reduces frizz, and provides lightweight hydration for long-lasting definition.
  4. Deep Moisture Hair Mask — An ultra-rich conditioning mask infused with nourishing oils and proteins, activated by H2O for deep hydration and silky softness.
  5. Volumizing Styling Mousse — A lightweight, airy mousse that adds lift and fullness from root to tip, enhancing body without stiffness while providing touchable, long-lasting volume.
  6. Flexible Hold Finishing Spray — A soft, workable finishing spray that sets styles in place while allowing natural movement, offering humidity resistance and a smooth, frizz-free finish.
  7. Extra Hold Sculpting Gel — A strong-control gel designed for sculpting, defining, and locking styles in place, delivering Level 4+ hold with a glossy, flake-free finish.
  8. Heat Protectant Mist — A protective spray that shields hair from heat damage up to 450°F, locking in moisture while preventing breakage during blow-drying, flat-ironing, or curling.
